My favourite games of all-time

Robert's top 3

The Last Guardian

The Last Guardian is a beautifully crafted and emotionally engaging video game that tells a heartwarming story of friendship between a young boy and a mysterious creature, taking players on an unforgettable journey through a breathtakingly designed world.

Mass Effect 2

Mass Effect 2 is an epic sci-fi RPG that offers an immersive and engaging experience, with a compelling storyline, unforgettable characters, and thrilling combat gameplay that keeps players on the edge of their seats from start to finish

Marvel Spider-man

Marvel's Spider-Man is a thrilling and action-packed adventure that allows players to step into the shoes of the iconic web-slinging superhero, swinging through the vibrant and beautifully detailed city of New York while battling an array of iconic villains and saving the day.
